#Regex

^ --> starting word of the statement
\ ---> followed by
w ---> Any alphanumeric word
+ ----> multiple occurance of alphanumeric word
. - ---> Dot or hypen or underscore
? ---> Multiple occurence of special cheracter(. _ -)
* ---> multiple occurance of combination of alphanumeric word and special character combined
@ --> this character should be present in email id
w{2,3} --> alphanumeric word with 2 to 3 character
$ --> ending word of the statement
